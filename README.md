# Customer-Churn-Revenue-Retention-Analysis
Our subscription revenue has plateaued despite steady new customer acquisition. Leadership suspects churn is eating into growth but we don't have clear visibility into who's churning, why, and what it's costing us. 
Deliverable: identify churn drivers, quantify revenue impact, and recommend 2-3 actionable retention interventions, backed by a dashboard leadership can monitor monthly.
Project deliverables :
1. What's our overall and segment-level churn rate, and is it trending up or down?
2. Which customer segments (plan type, tenure, usage level, acquisition channel) churn most?
3. What's the revenue impact of churn vs new acquisition (are we leaking more than we're gaining)?
4. What early warning signals predict churn (usage drop-off, support tickets, etc.)?
5. What should we do about it — which 2-3 interventions would have the highest ROI?
